Who We Are

We believe information should not be complicated.

BBT was built on the conviction that great ideas — business, branding, culture, technology — should be accessible to everyone. We translate complexity into clarity, so you can act on what you learn.

Every piece we publish is crafted to be intelligent without being intimidating, editorial without being elitist, and grounded in the real dynamics shaping markets and brands today.

M

Our Mission

To make business and brand knowledge genuinely accessible — removing the jargon, cutting through the noise, and delivering intelligence that empowers every reader.

V

Our Vision

A world where brand literacy and business acumen are universal — not exclusive to those in corner offices. We're building the media platform that gets you there.

Meet the Founder

Raunak
Pandey

Creator & Founder, BBT

Raunak Pandey built BBT from a simple frustration — that the best business and brand intelligence was locked behind complexity, jargon, and paywalls. With a deep passion for storytelling, brand strategy, and democratising access to knowledge, he set out to build a platform that speaks to every curious professional, not just those already inside the industry.

BBT is his commitment to the idea that intelligence should be a bridge, not a barrier — and that the best media respects your time as much as your intellect.

"Knowledge should empower everyone — not just those in boardrooms."
